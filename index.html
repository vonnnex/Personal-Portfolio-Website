About Me

Von Angela Cinco established the VAChain with a strong will and a great deal of determination. Her exceptional capacity for building relationships with various people and her long-term vision for overcoming short-term setbacks has allowed VAChain to continue grow into an innovative company. Von has a proven track record of producing graphic designs that aid companies in simplifying their processes and elevating the level of performance they deliver to their customers.

After graduating from Bukidnon State University, she began working for Upwork as a graphic designer. There, she was able to put her natural leadership skills as well as her creative thought process to use in order to devise new methods for enhancing project performance, thereby maximizing the potential of a diverse range of people working on the team to reduce the amount of time it took to complete tasks. Her remarkable people skills and her capacity to see beyond immediate obstacles are directly responsible for the transformation of VAChain into a forward-thinking business enterprise.
